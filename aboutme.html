Who Am I?

I'm Dechen Pelden

I am currently immersed in the exhilarating world of pursuing a Masters of Information Technology at Wentworth Institute of Higher Education. With a fervent passion for unraveling the mysteries of technology and its boundless applications, I find myself constantly intrigued by the ever-evolving landscape of IT.

Beyond the realm of ones and zeroes, I find solace and joy in the beautiful game of football. There's something about the sound of cleats striking the grass and the rush of adrenaline as I chase down the ball that makes me feel alive. Whether it's a friendly match with friends or a competitive showdown on the pitch, football never fails to ignite my spirit and bring a smile to my face.

My hobbies

When I'm not busy delving into the intricacies of coding or honing my football skills, you'll often find me lost in the captivating world of books. From gripping thrillers that keep me on the edge of my seat to thought-provoking philosophical works that challenge my perspective, I relish the opportunity to escape into the pages of a good book and explore new ideas and perspectives.

In essence, I'm a tech enthusiast, football aficionado, and avid reader, constantly seeking out new experiences and opportunities for growth. With a curious mind and a thirst for knowledge, I'm excited to embark on this journey of discovery and learning at Wentworth Institute of Higher Education.
